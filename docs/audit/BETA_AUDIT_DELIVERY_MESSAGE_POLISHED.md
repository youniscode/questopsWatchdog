# QuestOps Watchdog — Polished Beta Audit Delivery Messages

Tone: calm, professional, helpful, not pushy, no guarantees.

---

## 1. Short version (quick delivery)

> Hi [name],
>
> Your audit summary is ready. Here is a quick overview:
>
> **Overall status:** [PASS / FAIL / MIXED]
> **Key finding:** [One-line summary]
> **Quick fix:** [One quick win, if applicable]
>
> The full report is attached. Let me know if you have any questions.
>
> If you found this helpful, I would appreciate a short reply with your thoughts — takes 2 minutes.

## 7. Follow-up message after 3–5 days (no reply)

> Hi [name] — just a gentle check-in. I sent your audit summary a few days ago. If you had a chance to read it and have any questions, I am happy to help. If the timing is not right, no worries at all.

## 8. Testimonial request message (after positive feedback)

> I am glad the audit was helpful. If you are comfortable, I would appreciate a short quote I could use (anonymously or with attribution — your choice) to help others understand what the tool and audit service offer.
>
> Something as simple as: "[Your quote here]"
>
> No pressure at all — your feedback already helped a lot.

---

## Tone reminders

- Use "I" not "we" — keeps it personal and honest
- Lead with gratitude — the client's time and data are valuable
- Be clear about what the audit covers and does not cover
- Never pressure for a reply, feedback, or testimonial
- Never claim the tool or audit prevents all failures
- If you do not know something, say so

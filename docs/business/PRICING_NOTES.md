# QuestOps Watchdog — Pricing Notes

Internal reference for pricing rationale and strategy.

---

## Pricing tiers

| Tier | Price | Effort (hours) | Typical client |
|------|-------|----------------|----------------|
| Quick Scan | €49 | 1–2 | Single server, hobbyist |
| Standard Audit | €149 | 3–5 | 1–3 servers, small community |
| Comprehensive Audit | €299 | 6–10 | 4–10 servers, hosting provider |
| Enterprise Audit | €499+ | Custom | 10+ servers, SLA, dedicated contact |

## Rationale

- **Free tool first.** The tool is fully functional without paying. This builds trust and adoption.
- **Audit is a service, not a feature.** Clients pay for *human expertise*, not software access.
- **Low entry point (€49)** encourages first-time buyers. Low risk for the client.
- **Higher tiers** reflect real effort — reviewing 10 servers with config analysis and a follow-up call is genuinely time-intensive.
- **€499+ for enterprise** is deliberately vague. Large deployments vary wildly in scope; quote per engagement.

## Payment methods (suggestions)

- PayPal / Wise for individuals
- Invoice (Net 14) for businesses
- Consider 50% upfront for €299+ engagements

## When to discount

- **First client:** Offer a discount or do a free quick scan in exchange for a testimonial.
- **Non-profit / small community:** Consider 20–30% discount.
- **Repeat client:** Offer a loyalty discount (e.g., 10% off next audit).
- **Bundled audits:** 10% off when booking 3+ servers upfront.

## When to raise prices

- If you're consistently booked 2+ weeks out
- If client feedback consistently rates the audit as high-value
- Every 6–12 months as the tool and your expertise grow

## What to charge for (scope expansion)

These are *not* included in standard tiers but can be quoted separately:

- Emergency / same-day turnaround (+50%)
- Server migration or setup consultation (€50–100/hour)
- Custom script writing for specific game integrations (quote per script)
- Recurring monthly audit retainer (discounted per-audit rate)

## Currency note

Prices listed in EUR as a neutral default. Adjust to local currency as needed.
